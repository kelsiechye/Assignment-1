A 5-member group conssisting of Mitake Ran on guitar and vocals, Aoba Moca on Guitar, Uehara Himari on bass, Udagawa Tomoe on the drums and Hazawa Tsugumi on the keys. They debuted on September 6 2017 with the song This is How I Roll!

Background

Afterglow is a group formed by 5 childhood friends. When Ran moved to another class in middle school, the 5 girls made a band so that they could keep seeing each other. Now in highschool, they still love playing in their band, practicing after school and playing in live shows. Although they are still pretty inexperienced, their powerful performances and strong vocals have led them to gain popularity.
